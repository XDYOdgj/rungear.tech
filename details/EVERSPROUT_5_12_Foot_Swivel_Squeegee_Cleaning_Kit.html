EVERSPROUT 5-12 Foot Swivel Squeegee Cleaning Kit

Keep your windows sparkling clean with our efficient and eco-friendly window cleaning solution. Designed for both residential and commercial use, this multi-purpose kit offers a smooth, effortless way to clean even the toughest dirt and grime.

Get the Perfect Angle Every Time

With a swivel design that adjusts to 7 different angles, the EVERSPROUT squeegee makes cleaning bay, transom, or picture windows a breeze. This flexibility ensures you can reach every nook and cranny without strain or difficulty.

Lightweight and Durable Construction

Our lightweight aluminum pole is built for comfort and ease of use, featuring a non-slip grip that keeps your hands secure during long cleaning sessions. The 3-stage extension allows you to customize the length to suit your cleaning needs, reaching up to 20 feet without the need for ladders or unsafe positions.

Eco-Friendly and Cost-Effective

The washable microfiber scrubber pad is perfect for scrubbing away stubborn grime, and its reusable design helps you save money while reducing your environmental impact. This approachable cleaning solution is ideal for homeowners, renters, and anyone looking for a reliable, easy-to-use alternative to traditional window cleaners.

Built for Reliability

Featuring a 1.5-pound lightweight frame, the EVERSPROUT window squeegee is sturdy and built for long-term use. Its innovative flip-tab locking system prevents the common issues with twist-lock poles, ensuring a secure hold with every adjustment. Plus, enjoy peace of mind with our 1-year warranty and exceptional USA-based customer service.

Key Features

  • Swivel squeegee rotates to 7 angles for easy access
  • Lightweight aluminum pole with non-slip grip for comfort
  • 3-stage extension pole extends to any custom length
  • Washable microfiber scrubber pad for reuse and eco-friendly cleaning
  • 1-year warranty and exceptional USA customer service
